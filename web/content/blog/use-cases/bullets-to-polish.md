# Write Bullets, Let AI Polish: The Fastest Way to Create Content

Writing is slow because we try to do two things at once: **capture ideas** and **make them sound good.**

You're trying to think "What do I want to say?" while also thinking "How should I phrase this perfectly?"

**Your brain hates this.** It's why you stare at blank pages and rewrite the same sentence five times.

Here's a better way: **Separate the thinking from the polishing.**

Dump your ideas as messy bullet points. Let AI turn them into actual prose. You focus on what to say; Stash handles how to say it.

## The Writing Paralysis Problem

Most people get stuck because they're trying to write perfectly on the first try:

**The internal monologue:**

- "How should I start this?"
- "Wait, that doesn't sound right..."
- "Is this too informal? Too formal?"
- "Let me rewrite that intro..."
- _20 minutes later, still on paragraph one_

**The result:** Writing takes forever. You procrastinate. Documents that should take 20 minutes take 2 hours.

**The real problem:** You're editing while you're creating. That's like trying to drive with one foot on the gas and one on the brake.

## The Bullets-to-Polish Workflow

Here's how to write 10x faster:

### Step 1: Brain Dump as Bullets

Don't worry about sentences. Just capture the ideas:

```
Meeting with client - product feedback session
- They love the new dashboard
- Confused about pricing tiers (need better explanation)
- Requested bulk export feature
- Timeline: want it by end of Q1
- Budget approved for expansion
- Next steps: send proposal by Friday
- Follow-up call in 2 weeks
```

**No full sentences. No polish. Just raw ideas.** Takes 3 minutes.

### Step 2: Let Stash Polish

Tell Stash: "Turn this into a professional meeting summary."

Stash reads your bullets and generates:

```
Client Product Feedback Session - November 11, 2025

We met with the client team to review the new dashboard release.
Overall reception was extremely positive—they're excited about the
improvements and have already started using it with their team.

Key Feedback:
The team expressed some confusion around our pricing tier structure.
They understand the value but felt the distinctions between tiers
could be communicated more clearly. This is a good opportunity to
refine our pricing page and sales materials.

Feature Request:
The client requested a bulk export feature, which would significantly
improve their workflow for quarterly reporting. They emphasized this
is a priority and ideally need it by end of Q1 2026.

Budget & Next Steps:
Good news: budget has been approved for the expansion we discussed.
I'll send over the proposal by Friday as promised. We've scheduled
a follow-up call in two weeks to finalize details and address any
questions.
```

**Your 3 minutes of bullets became a professional doc in 30 seconds.**

## Why This Works So Well

**Speed:** Bullets are 5-10x faster than writing prose from scratch

**Focus:** You think about content, not formatting or phrasing

**Completeness:** Bullets ensure you capture everything before you forget

**Consistency:** AI applies consistent tone and structure

**Less editing:** Polished output needs light review, not heavy rewriting

**Lower barrier:** Much easier to start with bullets than a blank page

## How to Implement This Workflow

1. **Next time you need to write something, don't start with sentences**
2. **Brain dump bullet points instead** (capture everything quickly)
3. **Tell Stash what format you need:** email, meeting notes, blog post, etc.
4. **Review the polished output** (light edits only)
5. **Ship it** or send it

**The mental shift:** You're the thinker, AI is the polisher. Stay in your lane.
